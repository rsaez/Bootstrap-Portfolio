About Me

I like to build code that focuses on social habits. I am a very curious problem solver always looking for a challenge. My tech journey started with cybersecurity providing defense and offense in the cyberspace domain. Then as a consultant I design software solutions and fit them in existing datacenter infrastructures. When I code, I consider all of my experiences to deliver a user focused website.

I enjoy puzzles like Sudoku, board games like Settlers of Catan. I like to travel, you can always find me exploring remote places or new cultures abroad. I am an avid cooking student, my number one meal is chicken. I carry my camera everywhere I go sharing my perspective with the world. I take my inspiration from nature and love the outdoors.
